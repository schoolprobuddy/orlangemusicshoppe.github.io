Our story

Family-owned,
music-driven.

"8 years of sharing the love of music — thank you for making it all possible."

Orlange Music Shoppe was born from a simple dream: a place in San Pablo City where every musician — beginner or pro — feels at home.

We hand-pick every instrument on our shelves, give honest advice, and genuinely celebrate every milestone our customers reach. When you shop with us, you're family.

"A hidden gem in San Pablo City — knowledgeable, friendly, and passionate."
